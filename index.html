Areas of Expertise

How can I help you succeed?

Copywriting, Editing, PR

The English language has been a lifelong passion of mine. I love to read, write and edit. Whether it's copywriting, technical writing, policy writing, journalistic or creative, the written word has driven my career path for over two decades.

Design & Graphic Layout

As an expert user of the Adobe suite of programs, I love to make things look great -- from newspapers to magazines to e-magazines; from marketing collaterals to fully branded corporate packages. Whether in hard copy or digital format, each project represents a unique challenge.

Web Design & Social

I am familiar with a range of CMS platforms, Adobe Dreamweaver and even basic hand-coded HTML5 websites. Recently, I've added some Javascript to my skills, as well as the Adobe Muse web development tool.

Branding

You are only as good as your brand. I love logos. I love fonts. I love brands. I've had the opportunity to help a few companies establish their brands using creativity, skills and organization. I'd love to talk to you about yours!

Project Management & Events

As a Certified Associate in Project Management, I've learned to take the reins and ensure that projects under my portfolio are done properly, on schedule and according to specifications. I've also helped coordinate events from 25-800 people.

Photography. Video & Audio

Need a photo shoot? A video message? A podcast produced? Perhaps a Flash animation, or an interactive PowerPoint presentation or educational module? Call me!
